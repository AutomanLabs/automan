Use the Platform for Your Own Projects

You can use Automan Labs to plan and realize your own internal or client-facing projects — in a smart, automated way.

📝

Submit a request

You input a goal, idea, or need — even something vague like “we need a new dashboard for client X”.

🧠

Get a smart plan

The system analyzes your resources and generates a complete execution plan — timeline, assets, AI agents, partners.

🚀

Execute or delegate

You can execute internally, involve your team, or delegate to our trusted partners or autonomous AI workflows.

Example

A digital agency receives a last-minute client request. They submit it to the platform, which generates a plan, assigns AI tools, and selects an external partner to complete the visual part.
